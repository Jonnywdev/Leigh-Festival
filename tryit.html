TRYIT Sevens was set up and founded to help give the opportunity to young and aspiring players the chance to play a high level of sevens rugby. Our playing style is free to throw the ball around giving the chance for the players to enjoy rugby in a non-controlled environment. This year we are looking to grow by entering more elite tournaments and evolving our Leigh Festival sevens day, we are excited to confirm some of the top teams that will be coming and we will see you on the 27th of May.
