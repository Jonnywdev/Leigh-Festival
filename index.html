Who are we?

Leigh Festival was born out of the passion and dedication of a group of enthusiastic individuals, driven by their desire to bolster the financial support for Leigh RUFC and sustain grassroots rugby union in Leigh. None of our organizers receive any monetary compensation; they tirelessly pour their efforts into creating something truly remarkable for the entire Leigh community. Our aim is to provide an enjoyable experience for everyone while also giving local food vendors and shops an opportunity to showcase their talents.

As a team, we aspire to elevate the festival's growth, striving to make each year better than the last. However, as a small team with limited experience in event planning, we face challenges that demand considerable time and effort.

This is where we need your assistance! If you would like to be part of our team, please click the button below to explore the various roles available and find more information.

Sponsor Leigh Festival

Securing sponsorship for our event is crucial. We express our heartfelt appreciation for the individuals and companies that generously sponsored last year's Leigh Festival. Now, as we aim to expand the festival and foster greater inclusivity, we are actively seeking passionate individuals and forward-thinking companies to join us as sponsors.
By becoming a sponsor, you gain access to a range of exciting benefits, including:
• Exclusive invitations to our sponsors' evening, offering valuable networking opportunities with other sponsors.
• Weekend passes for you and your team, ensuring you don't miss a moment of the festival's excitement.
• Beer tokens, so you can indulge in our diverse selection of beverages throughout the event.
• The chance to engage with the local community, fostering brand awareness and leaving a lasting impact.
• Extensive promotion of your brand across all our social media channels, guaranteeing maximum exposure and visibility.

We firmly believe that your partnership will not only enhance our festival but also provide you with exceptional value and recognition. Join us on this incredible journey, and together, let's create an unforgettable experience for our attendees and a remarkable opportunity for your brand

TryIt Sevens Tournament

TRYIT Sevens was set up and founded to help give the opportunity to young and aspiring players the chance to play a high level of sevens rugby. Our playing style is free to throw the ball around giving the chance for the players to enjoy rugby in a non-controlled environment. Last year we had elite, social, ladies and under 14's tournaments which helped to evolve Leigh Festival sevens day!

Andy's man club

Andys Man Club is a peer on peer group, aimed to provide a safe, confidential environment for guys to come and talk

There's three types of men, those that are currently going through a storm, those that have come out the other side of a storm, and those that have a storm brewing on the horizon.

Walking through the door for the first time is the hardest thing to do, but once guys realise how relaxed it is, how there's no pressure to even talk, and that it's completely cool to just sit and listen, those guys leave feeling better for just coming through the door

Andys man club saved my life, any men are welcome, come down for a brew and a chat on Monday nights 7-9

We run every Monday apart from back holidays 7-9 at Leigh RUFC
