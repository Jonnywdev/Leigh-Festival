What is Leigh Festival?

Leigh Festival was created in 2020, with the first festival taking place over the jubilee bank holiday in 2022. The idea was to bring a fun event to Leigh which included live mucic from local bands and singers, a great mix of different beers, largers and ciders, rugby matches and much more!

Our aim is to give the community a great event right at their doorstep, without having to break the bank to see amazing live bands and singers and also to give people the opportunity to try different drinks they may not be able to find locally anywhere else.
