Without volunteers Leigh Festival would not exist!

Calling all passionate individuals and community champions! We are excited to announce the upcoming Leigh Festival, a celebration of art, culture, and community spirit. But we can't make it happen without your help! We are seeking dedicated volunteers to join us in organising this extraordinary event, and we invite you to be a part of this incredible journey.

By volunteering your time and expertise, you will play a vital role in creating a festival that will leave a lasting impact on our community. Here's why you should consider joining our team:

Organising Leigh Festival is not just about throwing a great event; it's about creating a platform where local talent can shine, fostering community connections, and showcasing the richness of our cultural diversity. Your contribution as a volunteer will directly impact the lives of artists, performers, and attendees, enriching our community in countless ways.

Volunteering for Leigh Festival offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in event management, marketing, logistics, and community engagement. You will have the chance to collaborate with experienced professionals, develop new skills, and broaden your network. This experience will be valuable for personal and professional growth, opening doors to future opportunities.

Working alongside like-minded individuals who share your passion for arts, culture, and community will create bonds that extend beyond the festival itself. You will have the chance to meet fellow volunteers, local artists, and community leaders, forging meaningful connections and building a network of support that can benefit you well beyond the festival.

We believe that everyone has unique talents and skills to contribute. Whether you excel in organisation, marketing, graphic design, logistics, or simply have a passion for community engagement, Leigh Festival provides a platform for you to showcase your abilities and make a tangible impact. Your expertise will be recognised, appreciated, and celebrated.

Leigh Festival is more than an event; it's a celebration of our community's vibrancy and resilience. By volunteering, you become an integral part of this remarkable experience, contributing to the fabric of our shared identity and creating memories that will be cherished for years to come. Together, we will create something truly extraordinary.

So, if you're ready to embrace this exciting opportunity and help us organise Leigh Festival, we warmly invite you to join our team. Your time, dedication, and passion will make a significant difference in shaping this incredible event. To volunteer or learn more, please visit our website, call our dedicated hotline, or attend one of our upcoming information sessions.
Thank you for considering this call to action. Together, let's create a festival that will inspire, unite, and bring joy to our community. Your involvement will make all the difference!
